Dashboard Guide

Getting Your Data

You can import your Lichess games directly via the API using your username, or by manually pasting a PGN file.

  1. Go to your Lichess Profile.
  2. Click the download icon (📥) at the top right of your games list to download your PGN.
  3. Click Upload PGN in the sidebar and paste the contents.

Using Filters

The filters at the top of the dashboard allow you to slice your data. Any changes to Time Control, Date Range, or Color will instantly update all charts and metrics on the current page.

Metric Glossary

  • Differential: The absolute difference between your win rates with White and Black.
  • Timeout Risk: The percentage of games you lose by running out of time.
  • Sweet Spot: The game length (in moves) where you have the highest win rate.
  • Longest Improvement: Your longest sustained period of rating gain without a dip.
